What is Pulseband?

The Pulseband is a wearable device that GE has created to measures a user’s vitals.

It is differentiated from standard sports fitness monitors by being specifically designed to also accommodate people with certain heart conditions.

About 600,000 people die of heart disease in the United States every year—that’s ONE in every FOUR deaths.

What can Pulseband do for you?

Track Progress

Keep Track of your sleeping habits, heart rate, blood pressure, and activity each day. The device turns different colors to indicate the wearer's progress/status based on their profile

Set Goals & Reminders

Receive Daily reminders on all the medications you have to take and set goals for yourself that you can accomplish each week.

Early Detection

Pulseband can detect irregular heart rhythms and alert you and the nearest hospital if you are having a cardiac episode.

Heart disease is the leading cause of death for both men and women.
